North8 Development Corporation

NORTH8 is a dynamic development initiative managed by North8 Development Corporation, built on a strong commitment to creating opportunities for SMEs and micro-entrepreneurs.

With a clear vision of inclusive growth, North8 develops prime commercial and residential spaces that support business shops, offices, service sectors, food districts, and community living.

Our Mission

North8 is managed by North8 Development Corporation. Our core mission is to create opportunities for (SME) Micro-Entrepreneurs by developing prime commercial and residential spaces that support business growth and community development.

We specialize in creating integrated environments that include business shops, offices, service sectors, food districts, and residential communities, all designed to foster success and growth.
